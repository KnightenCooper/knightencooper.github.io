Every Thursday at Getaway Golf: 50% Off for Students!

Getaway Golf is partnering with Springfield Public Schools to offer 50% off for all students. 4pm-10pm every Thursday. Getway Gold is located at 3833 E Sunshine.

April 10th-11th: Annual Show Me Gourd Art Festival

The Show Me Gourd Society is hosting their annual art festival for gourds. It's $5 a ticket and is located at 3001 N Grant Ave from 10am-5:30pm. Come by to look at all the gourd art or enter your gourd into the contest.

Every Monday at Galloway Station:

Every monday from 7pm-9pm, Galloway Station hosts a Irish Music night. Galloway Station is located at 1281 E Republic Rd. All Irish musicians are welcome to perform and anyone can come and enjoy the event. It's a great time to sing songs and enjoy the amazing food offered by Galloway Station.
