Springfield Bass Pro Shops History:

Johnny Morris, the owner of Bass Pro Shops, is proud to have grown up in Springfield, Missouri. The first store Johnny Morris opened in Springfield Missiouri was the only Bass Pro Shop for the first 13 years of business. Bass Pro Shops opened its first store in Springfield Missouri in 1972.

Springfield Art Museum History:

Opening in 1926, the Springfield Art Museum is Springfield's oldest building dedicated to culture. Deborah D. Weisel started an Art Study Club that was the beginnings of the art museum. The group changed their name to the Springfield Art Museum in 1928 and deeded the building to the city of Springfield.

Springfield Trail of Tears History:

Springfield was one of the places that the Trail of Tears went through. The Trail of Tears is the pathway that Cherokee people were forced to walk to get to Indian Territory in Oklahoma. The Cherokee people were forced to migrate from their homeland within Tennessee, Alabama, and Georgia. Springfield Missouri remembers the Cherokee people's travel by perserving portions of the Trail of Tears.
