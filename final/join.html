Our Purpose and Goals

Our purpose is to encourage businesses to operate in Missouri and result in there being more jobs, a larger workforce, and investments made into Springfield, Missouri. Our goals are to increase the number of businesses in Springfield, increase the amount of money invested into Springfield, and increase the number of quality workers in Springfield. We complete this goal by discussing economic development, setting up networking events, advocating for businesses, and assisting the workforce in securing employment.

Membership Levels

Non-Profit : $100

Non-profit members are given 1 directory listing and are allowed to attend luncheons and business trainings.

Bronze : $200

Bronze members are given 2 directory listings, get %50 off business trainings, and %50 off luncheon tickets.

Silver : $400

Silver members are given 5 directory listings, get 75% off business trainings, %75 off luncheon tickets, and can make 1 sponsored post using the Chamber of Commerce's social media a year.

Gold : $1000

Gold members are given unlimited directory listings, can attend all off business trainings and luncheons for free, and can make 1 sponsored post using the Chamber of Commerce's social media every month.
