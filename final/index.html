Our Purpose and Goals

Our purpose is to encourage businesses to operate in Missouri and result in there being more jobs, a larger workforce, and investments made into Springfield, Missouri. Our goals are to increase the number of businesses in Springfield, increase the amount of money invested into Springfield, and increase the number of quality workers in Springfield. We complete this goal by discussing economic development, setting up networking events, advocating for businesses, and assisting the workforce in securing employment.
