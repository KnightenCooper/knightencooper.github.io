Liang's Chinese Restaurant: Free Delivery!

Liang's Chinese Restaurant is happy to announce the they are offering free delivery to anyone who lives in Springfield. Delivery will be between 12pm and 4pm on business days and 9am to 9pm on Saturdays. Delivery is not offered on Sundays. Call (427) 316 - 9823 to get Liang's world famous Springfield Chicken or our classic Shrimp Chow Mein.

Green Eggs and Lawn: 1/2 Price Fertilizing!

Green Eggs and Lawn is running a special promotion on fertilizing. Dark green lawns can be acheived by using Green Eggs and Lawn's special fertilizer package. The fertilizer provides the nutrients that grass needs to turn dark green and grow. This fills in all of a lawns patches and is absolutely worth the money. Call (427) 491 - 2694 to reserve your space

Carl's Car Wash: Free Wax with Every Wash!

Carl's Car Wash is excited to announce that with every car wash customer's will enjoy a free car waxing. Adding wax after a car wash increases a car's shine, and protects the paint from weathering. Everyone who wants their car to look its best needs to go down to Carl's Car Wash and get a wash and wax. Carl's Car Wash is located at 3190 S. Ethan Street.
