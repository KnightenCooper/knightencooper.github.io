Target Audience:

People who have to go outside and live in Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven. The target audience has access to the internet and uses both mobile and PC to view websites

Persona #1: Bob McDoogal

Bob lives in Preston and is a teacher. He doesn't own a garage and has to walk to his car. The school bob works at has a parking lot that is a block away from the school. Bob likes planning and being prepared. He loves teaching and helping his students. Bob is 34 and teaches science. He wants a way to check and see if he will need his coat when he leaves the school building.

Persona #2: Sarah Boilal

Sarah is a stay at home mom. She has 3 children and loves them. Sarah's children love to go to the park. She loves taking them but struggles to know if they'll need jackets. Sarah uses her phone to look at websites. She believes that her goal in life is to raise her children and teach them how to be productive members of society. Sarah wants a website so she can check the current weather in Soda Springs where the park is.

Scenarios #1:

Someone needs to know what to wear for a event happening in Soda Springs. They live 5 minutes away and need to know the weather for a outdoor weddding.

Scenarios #2:

Someone wants to go sledding and lives in Preston. It isn't snowing in Preston but they are willing to drive to Soda Springs or Fish Haven. Before they drive, they want to check if it is snowing in Soda Springs or Fish Haven.

Scenarios #3:

Someone is about to leave work and needs to know whether or not to grab their jacket. They work in the middle of a building so there are no windows to look out. They want to know if they should take their jacket or leave it at their desk.

Scenarios #4:

Someone needs to know what to wear for a event happening in Preston. They live 5 minutes away and need to know the weather for a outdoor bonfire.

Scenarios #5:

Someone is about to go on a hike in Soda Springs. They want to know if they should bring a umbrella. Is it raining in Soda Springs? They ask the internet for answers.

Scenarios #6:

Someone needs to know what to wear for a event happening in Fish Haven. They live 5 minutes away and need to know the weather for a outdoor birthday party.
