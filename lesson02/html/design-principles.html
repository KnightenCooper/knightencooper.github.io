Visual Hierarchy

Visual Hierarchy

Company: Domino's Pizza

Link: https://www.dominos.com/en/

Description: Visual hierarchy is exemplified on the Domino's website because your eye sees the massive red "$599" and then “CHOOSE ANY 2 OR MORE” which causes the viewer to think they can get 2+ items for $5.99. In reality, each item costs $5.99 and some items are extra. The viewer doesn’t realize this because all the terms are on the bottom of the visual hierarchy.

Alignment

PARC: Alignment

Company: Pizza Hut

Link: https://www.pizzahut.com/

Description: PARC: Alignment is exemplified by the menu items on the Pizza Hut mobile website. Each menu item has an image and title. All six images are equally spaced and arranged in perfect alignment. The titles all start in the exact same space and are perfectly aligned both horizontally and vertically.

Contrast

PARC: Contrast

Company: Little Casears

Link: https://littlecaesars.com/en-us/

Description: PARC: Contrast is exemplified by Little Casears' website because of the equisite contrast between the soliid orange background and the white "$5 OFF YOUR FIRST DELIVERY". The high contrast between the orange and white make it easy to read and a great use of contrast.
