Lightning Strikes Turkey

During yesterday's rainstorm a family got a Thanksgiving suprise after lightning struck a turkey. The Milsons live next to the woods and often have wild animals that walk through their yard. Yesterday a turkey was walking through their yard when it was struck by lightning. The Melsons said they saw they saw the turkey get hit by the lightning and brought it inside. The turkey was fully cooked and according to the Melson's tasted like beef. Food scientists and professional chefs are now experimenting with using high voltage to make turkey meat taste beefier based on the Melson's experience.
